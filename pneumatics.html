Industrial Pneumatics
Industrial Pneumatics deals with the use of compressed air to execute mechanized tasks in industry. Pneumatics is used in those applications where high-speed movement (linear or rotary) with low to medium force is required. Where conventional AC/DC Motors and other electrical equipment canâ€™t be used or are less suitable, pneumatics is the technology of choice. Training in Industrial pneumatics aims to familiarize participants with the design, construction and operation of pneumatic components in industrial applications.
